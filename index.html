About Me

Welcome to my Portfolio website! I'm Robson, a Brazilian junior data analyst who's swapped the tropical beachs for the Canadian Winter.


My journey started in the legal field, where I worked mostly with contracts, as well as compliance of companies, what made me develop a detail-oriented mindset, strong communication skills, and the ability to manage complex and sensitive information. I transitioned into data analysis to merge my analytical skills with my passion for technology.


In my role as a Junior Data Analyst, I focus on transforming raw data into actionable insights. I design and generate detailed reports in Excel, handle comprehensive data gathering and preprocessing, and create interactive visualizations using Python libraries like Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Plotly. SQL is also a key tool in my toolkit, helping me efficiently extract, manipulate, and manage data to support robust analysis.


Thank you for visiting my Portfolio. Feel free to explore my work, and don't hesitate to reach out if you're intersted in know how I can contribute to your data-driven initiatives or company!
